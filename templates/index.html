About Us

A Hidden Gem in Kathmandu

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Kathmandu, Hidden Paradise Hotel offers a sanctuary of luxury and tranquility amidst the city's rich cultural tapestry.

Our boutique hotel seamlessly blends contemporary comfort with traditional Nepalese design elements, creating a unique atmosphere that honors local heritage while providing world-class amenities. Each space has been thoughtfully designed to offer an immersive experience that connects you with the soul of Nepal.
